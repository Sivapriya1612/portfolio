AFFILIATE MARKETING:

What is affiliate marketing?
How does it works??

Affiliate marketing is a marketing arrangement in which affiliates receive a commission
for each visit, signup or sale they generate for a merchant. This arrangement allows
businesses to outsource part of the sales process. It is a form of performance-based marketing
Affiliate marketers may use a variety of methods to generate these sales, including content marketing,
organic social media marketing and more.
